RGB game is a prediction game, that aims to actually be used as a mining game in future.

You have to your predictions by selecting three numbers, one number from each row, then click run button to match your discribtions.

You can only make predictions time is between 50 to 52 in an overall game. at the end of each game your earned score is converted to naira that you can cash out or use to purchase items or services on our site. you can start another game instantly

A session of the game is run three times after which you restart another session by clicking restart button.

After clicking restart button another session of the game will automaticaly start when you click run.

The game ends ofter game played reach between 50, 51 or 52.

If thetotal score is more than 50 you will earn naira else you will not earn anything.

If thetotal score is more than 50 the 50 will be deducted from the total score the remaing score will be multiply by #10 naira and you can cash out your money or use it on the site
